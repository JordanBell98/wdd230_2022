Redmond is a beautiful little town in the heart of Central Oregon.

Redmond draws a lot of people from all over due to its central loctaion.

Redmond's poplutation has been steadily growing since 1990 from 7,257 to 2021 at 35,582.

Redmond is close to all the action Smith Rock draws climbers from all over Oregon.

The Cove Palisades State Park is near by offering water sports.

While Mt. Bachelor is a short drive in the other direction offering extraordinary skiing to all. This is what Makes Redmond Oregon such a great place to visit.
